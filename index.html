About Me

Hello! I am Oscar Sefa Mireku, a passionate IT professional specializing in network administration, cloud computing, and enterprise infrastructure solutions. As a top graduate from NBCC’s IT Network Administration program and a Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA), I combine strong academic excellence with hands-on experience across Microsoft 365, Azure, AWS, Windows Server, and Cisco-based networking environments.
