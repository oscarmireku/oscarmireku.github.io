About Me

Hello! I am Oscar Sefa Mireku, a Network & Systems Professional and a Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA).

I am a results-driven IT professional specializing in the design, deployment, and management of secure enterprise infrastructure. As a graduate of the NBCC IT Network Administration program and a Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA), I bridge the gap between complex network engineering and user-focused technical support.

My approach combines a deep understanding of Cisco-based networking with a proficiency in Microsoft 365 and Hybrid Cloud environments. I don't just study technology—I build it. From maintaining a robust physical homelab with enterprise HPE servers to automating tasks with PowerShell, I am constantly refining the skills needed to keep modern businesses connected and secure.
